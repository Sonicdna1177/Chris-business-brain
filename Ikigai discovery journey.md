### Two Full SIV Courses
Simulated incidents in controlled conditions over water. Stalls, collapses, spirals, reserve deployment. You will know what your glider does — and what you do — when things go wrong.

### 3-Day Cross-Country Course
Mountain meteorology, route-reading, thermal navigation, and go/no-go decision-making in real terrain. The judgement framework that coastal training never gave you, applied in the Southern Alps.

### Competition Flying Module
Race tactics, task-setting, speed-to-fly, and the mental game of flying fast in a field. Competition training builds precision and situational awareness that recreational flying never demands.

### Vol-Biv Mission
Fly and camp. Navigate multi-day cross-country with overnight bivouac in the New Zealand backcountry. The ultimate test of autonomous judgement — no safety net, no crowd, just you, the terrain, and the decisions you make.

### Daily Debrief and Judgement Coaching
Every flight reviewed. Every decision examined. Chris reads what's beneath the surface — the habit, the hesitation, the pattern — and builds a personalised decision-making framework around your specific psychology and flying style.

### Full Digital School Access
Pre-brief, POV in-flight, and debrief video library for every SafePro manoeuvre. Use it before, during, and after the intensive as a permanent reference tool for everything you learn.

## Who This Is For

- You hold a PG2 or equivalent licence and you know something is missing — you just can't name it yet
- You trained coastally and want to fly mountains — safely, not hopefully
- You've had a moment in the air that scared you — and you want to understand exactly why
- You are ready to take complete ownership of every decision you make in the air
- You have a growth mindset, humility, and patience — and you understand that genuine skill takes time and attention

### This is not for you if:
- You want to tick a box and get a certificate as fast as possible
- You're not ready to accept full responsibility for your decisions in the air
- You are a beginner pilot — this intensive requires an existing PG2 or equivalent foundation
